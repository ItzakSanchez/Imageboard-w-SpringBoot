About

What is an image board?

An image board is an online platform where anonymous users can post and share images, often along with comments and discussions. It functions as a digital space where communities can gather around specific interests, topics, or hobbies. Users typically interact by posting images and engaging in conversations through text-based threads, which can be organized by categories or themes.

What is a board?

A board is the way an imageboard separates different topics. Each board is dedicated to a particular topic, such as art, technology, sports, etc. Users can post images and participate in discussions within that board, which usually has its own structure and rules related to content.

How to create a thread?

To create a new thread, follow these steps:
1) Enter the board you want to post to
2) Fill out the form
3) To create a new thread, it is necessary to upload at least 1 image. The maximum number of images per post is 4

How to reply to a thread?

To reply to a thread follow these steps:
1) Enter the thread you want to reply to, to do this, click on the thead number
2) Fill out the form
3) To create a new reply, no image is needed. The maximum number of images per post is 4
